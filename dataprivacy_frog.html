Privacy Policy | Frog Fly

Effective Date: December 16, 2025
Last updated: January 5, 2026

Antenna Games ("we," "our" or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled in "Frog Fly", our Android game available on Google Play.

Data We Collect

Our game does not collect any sensitive personal data.

The only data collected is:

  • Player ID, an automatically generated, anonymous, and unique number
  • Nickname (a name entered by the player inside the game)
  • User's in-game experience level
  • Highscores
  • Character appearance customization

The username does not need to be a real name and is not used to identify the player outside the game.

How We Use the Data

The collected data is used only to display leaderboards and show highscore information across players’ devices. The data shown on the leaderboards includes the player's username, experience level, highscore, and their in-game character's customized appearance.

Data Storage

The data is stored in an online database and is accessed only for leaderboard and gameplay functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Data Retention

Data is stored only as long as it is needed for leaderboard functionality.

How to Request Data Deletion

Players may request deletion of their game data by sending an email to antennagames.help@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”. Please note that this can only remove data stored in Antenna Games' databases; data collected by third-party services such as Google AdMob is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Local Data

The following data is stored only on your device and is not shared with our servers or third parties:

  • Your game settings
  • Your in-game character's appearance customization items you own

Children’s Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Frog Fly is not intended for children under 13.

Advertising

This game displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data such as advertising identifiers, cookies, and approximate location information to show personalized or non-personalized ads, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

For more information, please see Google’s Privacy Policy.

Personalized advertising is shown only with the user’s consent. Users can manage or withdraw their consent at any time through the in-app consent options.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at antennagames.help@gmail.com with the email subject line “Privacy Policy Question”.
