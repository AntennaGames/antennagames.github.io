Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 16th of December 2025

Antenna Games respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled in "Frog Fly", our Android game available on Google Play.

Data We Collect

Our game does not collect any sensitive personal data or device identifiers.

The only data collected is:

  • Username (a name entered by the player inside the game)
  • Highscores
  • Character appearance customization

The username does not need to be a real name and is not used to identify the player outside the game.

How We Use the Data

The collected data is used only to display leaderboards and show highscore information across players’ devices. The data shown on the leaderboards includes the player's username, highscore, and their in-game character's customized appearance.

Data Storage

The data is stored in an online database and is accessed only for leaderboard and gameplay functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data is stored only as long as it is needed for leaderboard functionality.

Players may request deletion of their data by contacting us at antennagames.help@gmail.com with the email subject line “Data Deletion Request”.

Children’s Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at antennagames.help@gmail.com with the email subject line “Privacy Policy Question”.
