Request Deletion of Your Data

Antenna Games ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data in Frog Fly, our Android game available on Google Play.

How to Request Data Deletion

Players may request deletion of their game data by sending an email to antennagames.help@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”. Please note that this can only remove data stored in Antenna Games' databases; data collected by third-party services such as Google AdMob is governed by their respective privacy policies. To help us locate your data, please include your in-game unique user ID in the message. You can locate your unique ID in the game's in-game settings screen.

Deletion of Local Data

You can delete local data saved on your device with a button in the game's settings menu.

Additional Information

See Antenna Games' Data Privacy Policy for more details on how user data is collected and handled.
