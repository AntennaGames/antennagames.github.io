The game was developed by Antenna Games and released November 15th of 2024.


Lake of Creatures takes inspiration from Nuclear Throne and The Binding of Isaac, and offers a fresh, but familiar roguelike experience to people who enjoyed those games. Featuring numerous classic roguelike features, such as randomized lakes, permadeath, randomized item drops and upgrades synergies, the game attempts to put a fresh spin on all its features. An unique fishing gimmick is also a central part of the gameplay loop. You cast the physics-based fishing line by swinging your mouse and clicking the cast-button near a fish. Cast enough times close to a fish and they will bite. In order to reel the fish in, it needs to be tired by succesfully timing clicks on a minigame after hooked. The game has over 200 unique fish!
