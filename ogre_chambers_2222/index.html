Antenna Games begun development of Ogre Chambers 2222 in 2025.

Ogre Chambers 2222 is a sequel to Ogre Chambers DX, a game from 2023 with over 500,000 plays on GX Games. The game tries to recapture the tight design of the original Ogre Chambers DX while adding features and decision making, to add depth to the originally simple but effective design loop. New additions include boss fights, ability finding and fusing to create extremely powerful weapons, careful choice making while choosing path the optimize the loot you get while maintaining the angriness of ogres to remain within your guns' capabilities.

Ogre Chambers 2222 will release in 2026.
