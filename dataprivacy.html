Antenna Games - Data Privacy Policy


Data Privacy Policy

Antenna Games' games on Google Play-store do not collect any kind of sensitive user and device data.

User's highscores, username* and their character's customized appearance are sent to a database, where they can be pulled from to show on the game on any player's devices if the player whose information was submitted has score high enough to reach the highscore table.

* User's username means a name the user has inputted inside the game.
